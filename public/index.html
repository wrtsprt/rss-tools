Feed Enrichment

Some RSS feeds only contain headlines or abstracts of the original article. It's only a limited amount of fun reading those in a news reader app (e.g. Reeder). The following feeds contain the content of the linked articles in the feed.

  • (German) www.heise.de/newsticker/heise-atom.xml => heise_newsfeed.rss
  • (Dutch) www.demorgen.be/category[/subcategory]/rss.xml => de_morgen.rss?1=nieuws&2=binnenland
